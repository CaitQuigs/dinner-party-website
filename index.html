How It Works

When you sign up for the Dinner Party Club, we match you with a group of other dinner party enthusiasts based on your location and preferences.

  • Join by yourself, with a significant other, or with a specific group
  • Number of members in the Dinner Party
  • Dietary Requirements - allergies, preferences, etc.
  • Are you adventurous? Do you want to try a variety of different cuisines?

Sounds good to you? Start your sign up here!
