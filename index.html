Do you like dinner parties?

Join Dinner Party Club!

Everybody loves dinner parties! Meet new friends and explore new cuisines at the Dinner Party Club!

  • Do you like to cook?
  • Do you like to eat?
  • Do you enjoy spending time around a dinner table with friends?

If you answered yes to these questions, then you should definitely join the Dinner Party Club!

How It Works

When you sign up for the Dinner Party Club, we match you with a group of other dinner party enthusiasts based on your location and preferences. You can join by yourself, with a significant other, or with a specific group.

  • Number of members in the Dinner Party
  • Dietary Requirements - allergies, preferences, etc.
  • Are you adventurous? Do you want to try a variety of different cuisines?
